Book as a Fossil
(Book, 2018)

Book as a Fossil is a compilation of visual elements from 10 different books.
It was established out of a need to show that books in our society became something of a shape of a fossil. It preserves not only content; so thoughts, feelings and ideas of an author, but also graphic style and aesthetics of the moment it was written in. In this book, we tried to show books as a fossil.

Made in collaboration with Anastasia Umpeleva, Agata Markowski and Martin Menso

Sound of Liberation
(Book, 2020)

This book is based on a poster of Sound of Liberation. Sound of Liberation was Korean cultural & speaking concert tour arond Europe. The concert was aimed at informing the world of a message that prayed for the unification of two Koreas and refused to be seperately registered with the UN. The used text is The First Korean Cultural & Speaking Tour in Europe, Sound of Liberation by Yongtak Choi.
