Eunjin Ahn (안은진) is a graphic designer & illustrator from Seoul, South Korea, currently studying BA Graphic Design at Royal Academy of Art the Hague (KABK).

She loves to blur the boundaries between analogue and digital, and trying to find the greatest freedom and fun within the limits given in a virtual space.
